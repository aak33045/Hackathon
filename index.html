FCCLA

FCCLA stands for Family, Career, and Community Leaders of America.

FCCLA members have been making a difference in families, careers, and communities by focusing on personal, work, and societal issues. Members partcipate in community service opportunities, leadership, conferences. By joining, members can develop real world skills and experience different career pathways. Addtionally, through FCCLA, members may be able to apply for scholarships.
